Circles of Care

Tech Specs

 

Circles of Care is an unassisted interactive installation with 2 possible settings:

 

A: Roof to Ground (Preferred): There's a short throw projector  that hangs at least 9 feet above the ground to create an 11’-0” diameter circular projection on the floor.

 

There’s also a (optional)  6’-0” diameter circular screen hanging 7’-0” from the ground. In the middle of the space there’s a sphere in a stand that works as input for the participants. Depending on the conditions of the space, the projector can be placed higher or lower to create a bigger or smaller area of installation.

 

B: Ground to Wall: In rooms where there's not enough height to hang the projector, it can be placed in the ground pointing to a wall that is at least 8 feet away from the projector.

 

Finally, there's a third element that depends on the Fire Marshall clearances of the space:  An optional fog machine that enhances the experience of the participants creating a cone of light.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 INSTALLATION SETUP

 

Technology Involved:

  • Game Ready(GTX 1070 or 1080 Graphics card or equivalent) Windows Gaming PC with a TouchDesigner Commercial License and Temporal Internet connection for Setup
  • Makey Makey Go
  • 2 Power Strips
  • 1 50’-0” Extension Cord
  • 1.5 Throw or Less Projector (I use an Epson PowerLite 535W)
  • 5.1 or Stereo Speakers Setup

 

Installation Pieces Involved:

  • 11’-0” Diameter Light Grey Exhibition Decal Circle on the Floor
  • 3’-6” Gazing Ball Pedestal
  • 12” Stainless Steel Garden Gazing Ball
  • 6’-0” Diameter Overhead PVC Hoop with Sheer Projection Fabric
  • Ceiling Projector Mount
  • 4 Overhead Hoop Hanging Points
  • Fog Machine

 

Electrical requirements

  •  3 120 V  lines @ 5 amp each

About

Variations of Nowhere is a VR installation that reflects about impossible places. Taking the spectator through un-existing spaces the piece is an inquiry about where are the spatial limits of what we call reality. A modified cave, a liquid room and floating structure in the middle of an infinite sky are the vehicles of reflection that drive Variations of Nowhere.

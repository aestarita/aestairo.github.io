About

Ana Estarita Guerrero (b. 1988) is a Colombian Immersive Media Artist with a talent for creating fantastic and eerie environments using visuals that inhabit the space between the organic and the digital. Her work combines analog liquid light painting with digital processing to create colorful living spaces that are rendered through Cinematic VR, Projection mapping, Mixed Reality or Interactive Installations

 

Her projection mapping and installation work have been featured in multiple shows and festivals including Animafest Zagreb, the Museum of Contemporary Art of Zagreb, The Expose Fair at Ottawa Int’l Animation Festival, Rhythms & Visions/ Expanded live, Getty Unshuttered and Getty Unshuttered 2, in DIG Showcase at Slamdance, and Femmebit.

 

She obtained her M.F.A in Animation and Digital Arts at the University of Southern California where she was awarded the prestigious International Artist Fellowship. Since 2017 Ana Carolina joined the core team of GLAS Animation Festival and since 2018 joined the festival as a Programmer and Head of Exhibitions. In 2019 was invited as a Programmer of Slamdance’s DIG.

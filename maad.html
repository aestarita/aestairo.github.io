About

MAAD is an Augmented Reality Experience in which the plater meets an energetic little alien whose eagerness to show off its secret world initiates a crisis that only the player can stop.

 

The project, developed for Microsoft hololens, rests at the World Building Media Lab at the School of Cinematic Arts of the University of Southern California. The project uses Augmented reality, installation and lighting to create an immersive experience.

 

The project was developed with Ann Lee as a producer, Marryyan Landlord as Interactive Lead and Daniel Ma as animation Lead. I developed the virtual and physical environments taking care of elements as the lighting, the materials, the general construction and the integration and animation of virtual elements.

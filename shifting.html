About

Shifting and G22 were commissioned by Patternson+Reckinger for Rhythms + Visions / Expanded + Live 3 event. The two 15 seconds visual music piece were exhibited on a 70 foot wide screen on the USC Cinematic Arts lawn.

 

Later on, Shifting was screened as part of Animafest Zagreb 2017 selection of site specific animation competition: Animafest goes MSU.

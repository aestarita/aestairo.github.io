GETTY UNDERWORLD

Getty Underworld was an Immersive Projection Exhibit Directed and Curated By Mike Patterson and Candace Reckinger that took place in the historic Getty Villa in Malibu. We used projection mapping in more historic buildings buildings and mapped a 4.000 year old Jupiter sculpture at the center of one of the exhibition rooms.
‍

I designed and directed the lighting scheme across the event. Designed and programmedin Touchdesigner the projection mapping system used for the event. Finally, I also worked as a content creator creating dynamic and colorful 3D animations that were projected on inner peristyle of the beautful Getty Villa.

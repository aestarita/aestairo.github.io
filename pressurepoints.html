PRESSURE POINTS

Pressure Points is a physical thinking exercise that reflects about how our notions of space and aesthetics are defined by the tools we use to think our surroundings. The piece tries to generate a pre-cartesian experience of space. A experience where the body itself is the plane of reference in a movable, irregular layout.

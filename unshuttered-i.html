GETTY UNSHUTTERED I

Getty UnShuttered was an Immersive Projection Exhibit Directed and Curated By Mike Patterson and Candace Reckinger with over 3000 attendees. We used projection mapping in more than 10 buildings and rock fountain at The Getty Unshuttered, featuring young photographers.

I created dynamic and colorful 3D animations that were projected on the rocks and buildings of the Getty center. I also worked with the artist Akiko Yamashita in the 3D scanning of the rocks used as template for the other artists
